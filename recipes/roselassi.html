Rose Lassi

Rose Lassi is one of the most popular flavours of Lassi, which is a curd based cool drink enjoyed by people all over the Indian subcontinent. It is really simple to make and yet unbelievably tasty.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups dahi (plain, unsweetened curd)
  • 0.5 cup rose syrup
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • ice
  • water to adjust consistency

Steps

  1. Take a blender
  2. Add all the ingredients into the blender jar
  3. Blend until the mixture becomes lump free and smooth.
  4. Top with rose petals and nuts if you wish.
  5. Serve cold.
